# Code of Conduct

Aethrion follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) principles in spirit: be respectful, constructive, and focused on making the project better.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or imagery.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or project work.

## Reporting

If a problem occurs, open a private channel with the project maintainer rather than escalating in public threads. Reports will be handled with care and context.
